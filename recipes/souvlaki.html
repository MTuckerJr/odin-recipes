Chicken Souvlaki

Description

Ill never forget the first time I experienced Souvlaki. I was pretty new to mediterranean food all together. It was juicy, tangy, and flavorful. I add this to my favorite list simply because it is an unforgettable experience.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb chicken thigh fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves , minced
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 - 3/4 tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Flatbreads/wraps/pita bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
