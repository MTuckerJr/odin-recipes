Turkey Burger

Description

I used to think a real beef burger was the only way. This juicy piece of lean meat changed my mind. To top its off its healthy aswell. I promise just one wont cut it.

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey, dark meat preferred or 80-90% lean
  • Kosher salt
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Italian herb mix
  • Garlic
  • Red Onion
  • Liquid smoke
  • Egg
  • Panko
